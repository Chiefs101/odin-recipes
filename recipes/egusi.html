Egusi Soup

Egusi (Melon in English language) soup is a one of the best dishes in West Africa and it is very easy to prepare , having egusi soup along side iyan or fufu or eba or semo will make you experience heaven on earth. DontDull

Ingredients

  • Raw Egusi
  • salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • meats
  • fishes
  • Palm Oil
  • Onions

Steps

  1. boil the meat and fish
  2. fry the Egusi
  3. boil the pepper
  4. Add the egusi
  5. Add Meats , fish and vegetables
  6. then let it boil for a few minutes
